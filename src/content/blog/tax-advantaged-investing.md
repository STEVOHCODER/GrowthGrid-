Tax efficiency is just as important as investment returns. If you're not optimizing your tax buckets, you're leaving money on the table.

## The Three Buckets of Taxation
1. **Taxable:** Brokerage accounts and savings.
2. **Tax-Deferred:** Traditional 401(k) and IRA.
3. **Tax-Free:** Roth IRA and HSA.

## Why the HSA is a Secret Wealth Weapon
The Health Savings Account (HSA) offers a triple tax advantage: contributions are tax-deductible, growth is tax-free, and withdrawals for medical expenses are tax-free. By 2026, the contribution limits have adjusted, making this an even more powerful vehicle for long-term growth.

## Strategic Rebalancing
Review your portfolio quarterly to ensure your asset location matches your tax strategy. High-dividend stocks should generally stay in tax-advantaged accounts.
