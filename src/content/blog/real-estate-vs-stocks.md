The age-old debate continues. In 2026, interest rates have stabilized, creating a unique environment for both asset classes.

## Real Estate: The Power of Leverage
Real estate allows you to control a large asset with a small amount of capital. 
* **Pros:** Monthly cash flow, tax depreciation, and physical security.
* **Cons:** Illiquid, requires management, and high entry costs.

## Stocks: The Power of Liquidity
Dividend growth investing (DGI) remains the most accessible way to build passive income.
* **Pros:** Zero management, instant liquidity, and low barrier to entry.
* **Cons:** Market volatility and no physical control.
