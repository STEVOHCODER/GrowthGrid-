In a rapidly changing economy, liquidity is king. However, keeping too much cash in a low-interest savings account can cost you thousands in lost opportunity.

## The New 'Tiered' Emergency Fund
Instead of one lump sum, consider a three-tier approach:
1. **Tier 1:** 1 month of expenses in a standard checking account (Instant access).
2. **Tier 2:** 2-3 months in a High-Yield Savings Account (HYSA).
3. **Tier 3:** 3+ months in short-term T-Bills or Money Market Funds.

## Inflation Protection
With inflation still a factor in 2026, Tier 3 assets should ideally keep pace with CPI to ensure your purchasing power doesn't erode while your money sits "safe."
